# Aristocrat Website Redesign

*A web presence redesign I prototyped and developed for Aristocrat—a global leader in gaming content and technology.*

**Prototype & Web Development**

[Visit Live Site](https://www.aristocrat.com/)

---

## The Brief

### Elevating a Global Gaming Brand

Aristocrat, a world leader in gaming content and technology, needed to revamp their web presence. The goal was to create a top-tier digital experience showcasing their innovation while making content management straightforward for their team.

The challenge: deliver an engaging, scalable website that could evolve alongside the brand's growth.

---

## What I Delivered

### Collaborative Prototype-to-Production

I worked alongside Aristocrat's stakeholders and in-house designers, leading the prototyping and full frontend development.

**Design**

- Wireframe prototyping in collaboration with in-house team
- Interactive elements including videos, dynamic icons, and animated text
- Flexible design system adaptable to evolving brand colours and layouts

**Development**

- Custom frontend build (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
- Content Management System integration for easy updates
- Interactive scrolling and visual elements
- Scalable architecture for future growth
