## The Brief

### Digital Home for a World-Class Academy

The Academy Byron Bay is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu facility led by world champion black belt Thalison Soares (200k+ followers). They needed a website that reflected their world-class coaching while making it easy for new students to explore classes, book trials, and understand what makes the academy different.

The challenge: create a clean, professional presence that matches the academy's elite reputation while keeping the site easy for the team to update.

---

## What I Delivered

### End-to-End in ~24 Hours

I designed and built the complete website across approximately 24 hours of work over 4 weeks.

**Design**

- Clean visual identity reflecting elite coaching and welcoming atmosphere
- Clear user journeys for new students, trial sign-ups, and memberships
- Mobile-optimised layouts for on-the-go access

**Development**

- WordPress custom theme (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP)
- Simple custom CMS for easy content updates
- Responsive design across all devices
- Performance optimisation for fast loading
