<Hgroup sectionId="overview">			
    
    <p slot="section">Overview</p>
    
    <h2 slot="body">Revitalising Ticketing Professionals Australia's Online Presence</h2>			
    
    <p slot="body">Ticketing Professionals Australia, founded in 2017, aims to enhance professional growth and understanding in the Asia Pacific region's ticketing industry. To support their mission, the organisation needed a dynamic new website that would showcase their annual conferences, sponsors, and donors, while offering a seamless user experience for diverse audiences.</p>
    
    <p slot="body">Antonio, in his role as Lead Designer and Developer, spearheaded the UX/UI design and development to create a highly visual and user-friendly platform that clearly communicates key information and highlights the conference’s year-by-year evolution.</p>

</Hgroup>

<Hgroup sectionId="takeaways">			

    <p slot="section">Takeaway</p>
    
    <h2 slot="body">Showcasing Conference Highlights</h2>
    
    <p slot="body">The redesign prioritised visually engaging elements to effectively highlight each year’s conference, sponsors, and donors, ensuring the website serves as a central hub for all event-related information.</p>
    
    <h2 slot="body">Clear User Pathways</h2>
    
    <p slot="body">The new site structure was designed with intuitive navigation, providing a straightforward path for users to quickly find the information they need, such as event details, registration, and speaker information.</p>
    
    <h2 slot="body">Enhanced Audience Engagement</h2>
    
    <p slot="body">By focusing on a highly visual design, the site captures the dynamic nature of the ticketing industry and encourages interaction, fostering stronger connections with attendees, sponsors, and donors.</p>

</Hgroup>
