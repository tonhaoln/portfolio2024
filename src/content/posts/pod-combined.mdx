<Hgroup sectionId="problem">

    <p slot="section">The Problem</p>

    <h2 slot="body">15 Minutes to 30 Seconds</h2>

    <p slot="body">Print on Demand sellers manually compare profit margins across providers using spreadsheets. Each calculation takes 15-20 minutes and becomes outdated as pricing shifts.</p>

    <p slot="body">PODCombined delivers real profit margins across 100+ provider-product-region combinations in under 30 seconds.</p>
    
</Hgroup>

<Hgroup sectionId="how-built">

    <p slot="section">How It Was Built</p>

    <h2 slot="body">Design → Development → Deployment in 3 Weeks</h2>

    <p slot="body"><strong>Design</strong></p>

    <ul slot="body">
        <li>User interface and comparison tool layouts</li>
        <li>Input flows and result visualisation</li>
        <li>Responsive design across devices</li>
    </ul>

    <p slot="body"><strong>Development</strong></p>

    <ul slot="body">
        <li>Frontend (React, Next.js)</li>
        <li>Data scraping and JSON processing</li>
        <li>Multi-currency support and conversion</li>
        <li>Deployment to production</li>
    </ul>

    <p slot="body"><strong>Approach:</strong> AI-accelerated development with design foundations first. Component architecture and UX patterns created stable constraints before implementation. Technical decisions favoured simplicity: CSV files over databases, USD-only storage with display conversion, proven stack over experimental tools.</p>
