<Hgroup sectionId="brief">

    <p slot="section">The Brief</p>

    <h2 slot="body">Digital Presence for Social Impact</h2>

    <p slot="body">HomeGround Real Estate Sydney addresses the housing crisis by providing rental homes for essential workers, families under housing stress, and the "hidden homeless." They needed a website that clearly communicated their mission while guiding different audiences—property owners, tenants, and developers—toward relevant actions.</p>

    <p slot="body">The challenge: create an engaging, visually dynamic site that balances social impact messaging with practical user journeys.</p>

</Hgroup>

<Hgroup sectionId="delivered">

    <p slot="section">What I Delivered</p>

    <h2 slot="body">Clear Journeys with Dynamic Visual Elements</h2>

    <p slot="body">I led the UX/UI design and frontend development, creating a site that uses visual storytelling to communicate HomeGround's mission while keeping navigation intuitive.</p>

    <p slot="body"><strong>Design</strong></p>

    <ul slot="body">
        <li>Large image tiles clearly defining customer journeys for tenants, landlords, and developers</li>
        <li>Strategic calls to action encouraging engagement</li>
        <li>Custom graphics including wallpaper inspired by the logo's house graphic</li>
        <li>Cohesive branding unifying all sections</li>
    </ul>

    <p slot="body"><strong>Development</strong></p>

    <ul slot="body">
        <li>Custom frontend build (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)</li>
        <li>Dynamic text and image elements with entry animations (left/right margins meeting centre)</li>
        <li>Responsive design across all devices</li>
        <li>Performance optimisation</li>
    </ul>
