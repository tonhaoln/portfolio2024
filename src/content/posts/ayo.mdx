<Hgroup sectionId="brief">

    <p slot="section">The Brief</p>
    
    <h2 slot="body">Three Distinct Audiences, One Website</h2>			
    
    <p slot="body">Australian Youth Orchestra needed a website redesign serving three user groups: prospective participants and families researching programs, concert-goers booking tickets, and donors seeking impact transparency. Each audience had different goals and required clear pathways without competing for attention.</p>

    <p slot="body">The challenge: balance immediate clarity for each audience while maintaining a unified brand experience that reflects the orchestra's national prestige and artistic excellence.</p>

</Hgroup>
