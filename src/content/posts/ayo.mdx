<Hgroup sectionId="overview">			
    
    <p slot="section">The Brief</p>
    
    <h2 slot="body">Three Distinct Audiences, One Website</h2>			
    
    <p slot="body">Australian Youth Orchestra needed a website redesign serving three user groups: prospective participants and families researching programs, concert-goers booking tickets, and donors seeking impact transparency. Each audience had different goals and required clear pathways without competing for attention.</p>

    <p slot="body">The challenge: balance immediate clarity for each audience while maintaining a unified brand experience that reflects the orchestra's national prestige and artistic excellence.</p>

</Hgroup>

<Hgroup sectionId="takeaways">			

    <p slot="section">What I Delivered</p>
    
    <h2 slot="body">Strategic Navigation with Immersive Visual Storytelling</h2>
    
    <p slot="body">Led full redesign at Emptyhead Digital—from discovery and wireframes through UI design and development—creating an immersive digital experience that serves three distinct user groups.</p>
    
    <p slot="body"><strong>Design</strong></p>

    <ul slot="body">
        <li>Discovery research and persona development</li>
        <li>User journey mapping for three audience types</li>
        <li>Wireframes and interactive prototypes</li>
        <li>UI design system and component library</li>
    </ul>

    <p slot="body"><strong>Development</strong></p>

    <ul slot="body">
        <li>WordPress custom theme (HTML, SCSS, JS, PHP)</li>
        <li>Custom mega menu with audience-specific pathways</li>
        <li>Video mask entrance animation</li>
        <li>Parallax scroll effects for visual immersion</li>
    </ul>
