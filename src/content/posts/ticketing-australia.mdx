<Hgroup sectionId="brief">

    <p slot="section">The Brief</p>

    <h2 slot="body">Central Hub for a Professional Community</h2>

    <p slot="body">Ticketing Professionals Australia supports professional growth in the Asia Pacific ticketing industry. They needed a website redesign that would showcase their annual conferences year-by-year, highlight sponsors and donors, and serve diverse audiences—from conference attendees to potential sponsors.</p>

    <p slot="body">The challenge: create a visually engaging site that organises conference history clearly while guiding different user types toward relevant information.</p>
