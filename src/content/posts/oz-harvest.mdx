<Hgroup sectionId="brief">

    <p slot="section">The Brief</p>

    <h2 slot="body">Communicating Impact Through Interactive Storytelling</h2>

    <p slot="body">OzHarvest needed their 2023 impact report to do more than list statistics. They wanted an engaging digital experience that would bring their achievements to life for stakeholders, partners, and donors—showing the human impact behind the numbers across their four pillars: Feed, Educate, Advocate, and Innovate.</p>

    <p slot="body">The challenge: balance substantial content delivery with an experience that keeps users engaged and makes complex data accessible.</p>

</Hgroup>

<Hgroup sectionId="delivered">

    <p slot="section">What I Delivered</p>

    <h2 slot="body">Visual-First Design with Clear Navigation</h2>

    <p slot="body">I led the design and development at Emptyhead Digital, creating an interactive microsite that prioritises visual storytelling while managing a large volume of content.</p>

    <p slot="body"><strong>Design</strong></p>

    <ul slot="body">
        <li>Information architecture for content-heavy storytelling</li>
        <li>Visual hierarchy using imagery and statistics as focus points</li>
        <li>Clear navigation options allowing users to skip or explore at their own pace</li>
        <li>Responsive design for all devices</li>
    </ul>

    <p slot="body"><strong>Development</strong></p>

    <ul slot="body">

        <li>Interactive scrolling elements and animations</li>
        <li>Performance optimisation for efficient loading</li>
        <li>Web accessibility compliance</li>
    </ul>
