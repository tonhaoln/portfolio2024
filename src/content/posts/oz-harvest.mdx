    <h2 slot="body">Microsite Design and Development</h2>			
    
    <p slot="body">In 2023, OzHarvest celebrated another year of remarkable achievements by delivering much more than just food to people in need across the country.</p>

    <p slot="body">The design of their 2023 impact report microsite, led by Antonio in his role as Lead Designer Partner at Emptyhead Digital, aims to share these achievements and the impact of their work in feeding, educating, advocating, and innovating with stakeholders, partners, and customers.</p>

<Hgroup sectionId="takeaways">			

    <p slot="section">Takeaway</p>
    
    <h2 slot="body">A Visual and Interactive Journey</h2>
    
    <p slot="body">The initial challenge centred on prioritising a visual and interactive experience, crucial for vividly showcasing impact statistics and project highlights.</p>
    
    <h2 slot="body">Easy Navigation</h2>
    
    <p slot="body">Managing the delivery of a substantial amount of content was a pivotal task, achieved by establishing focus points with imagery and statistics, and by offering users clear options to skip or navigate through content seamlessly.</p>
    
    <h2 slot="body">Inclusive User Experience</h2>
    
    <p slot="body">OzHarvest's commitment to its broad audience reinforced the adherence to best practices during development, ensuring the website's efficient performance and compliance with web accessibility standards.</p>
